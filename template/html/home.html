How It Works

  1. Select an environment image from our pre-configured options.
  2. Click 'Launch' to create your new environment.
  3. Access your environment through the web interface or terminal.
  4. Work on your projects with all the tools you need
  5. When finished, easily delete the environment to free up resources.
